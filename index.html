Welcome to a design where culture and mathematics come together in harmony. This roundabout is more than just a roadway feature. It is a symbol of unity, identity, and community. Inspired by the beloved Pancit Cabagan of Cagayan, a dish that brings families and friends together, the circular form reflects connection and togetherness. Just as the long noodles intertwine and gather in one serving, the roads in this design meet at a shared center, the heart of the community. At the same time, mathematics gives the structure balance and precision. Through the use of circles, symmetry, radius, diameter, and circumference, the design becomes both meaningful and mathematically accurate. By blending tradition with geometry, this roundabout warmly represents how culture and numbers can beautifully shape the spaces we share.

Design Concept

This roundabout design was created to improve traffic safety while enhancing the aesthetic beauty of Tuguegarao City. Its circular form represents balance, unity, and the continuous flow of movement within the community.
